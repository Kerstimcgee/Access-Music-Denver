Access Music Denver

Connecting students in need with classical, private music instructors.

A future app that enhances a widespread value for high arts across United States culture through deeming private music lessons accessible to all of our children.
  • Most families cannot afford private music lessons, therefore a lot of musical talent never finds its way into fruition. Let's change this.
  • It is proven that young people involved in the rigor of classical music learning processes are more likely to be successful adults and creators to better our society.
  • Instigating access for all children to have this opportunity will proactively influence the minds of the up and coming human race.
